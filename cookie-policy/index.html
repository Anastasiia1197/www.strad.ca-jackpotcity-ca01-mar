Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our site. They help us to:

  • 🗂️ Remember your preferences - Language, regional settings, and display options
  • ⚡ Improve performance - Faster page loading and optimized navigation
  • 📊 Analyze usage - Understand how visitors interact with our content
  • 🎯 Personalize content - Tailor information to your interests
  • 🔒 Enhance security - Detect suspicious activity and prevent fraud

Take Control: How to Easily Customize Your Cookie Preferences

You have total control over the cookies used on https://www.strad.ca/:

Management via Our Consent Banner

During your first visit, a banner allows you to choose which types of cookies you accept. You can modify your preferences at any time by accessing our privacy settings.

Configuring Your Browser

You can also configure your browser to refuse all cookies or to alert you when a cookie is sent:

  • 🌐 Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • 🦊 Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • 🅰️ Microsoft Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Cookies
  • 🍎 Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data

Important: Disabling certain essential cookies may affect normal functionality and limit your user experience.
